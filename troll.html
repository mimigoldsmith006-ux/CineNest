Trollhunters: Rise of the Titans

1h 45min

Genre: Adventure

The Guardians of Arcadia reunite to battle the nefarious Arcane Order, who've reawakened the primordial Titans.

Movie Reviews-

guillermodeltoro ⭐️10/10



Del Toro's passion project and it shows in every single frame. The world building is extraordinary, the characters are richly developed and the stakes feel genuinely real. Jim Lake Jr is one of the best animated protagonists in years.

parentreview ⭐️7/10



My kids are obsessed with this and honestly I got pulled in too. It starts a little slow but once the troll world opens up it becomes completely gripping. The friendship between Jim and Toby is genuinely heartwarming.
