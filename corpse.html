2005

Corpse Bride

1h 17min

Genre: Musical

A shy young man inadvertently brings a deceased woman to life when he practises his wedding vows near her grave. She thinks that she is his wife and takes him to the land of corpses.

Movie Reviews-

halloweenfan ⭐️7/10



Perfect Halloween viewing. Spooky enough to feel atmospheric but never actually scary. The songs are catchy and the voice cast is wonderful. Not quite as deep as Nightmare Before Christmas but gorgeous and charming in its own right.

darkfairytale ⭐️8/10



A beautifully melancholy love story that manages to be funny and touching at the same time. Emily the Corpse Bride is such a sympathetic character and her ending is genuinely moving. The skeleton jazz band alone is worth the price of admission.
